Structural design

Structural design in construction is a critical aspect of the building process. It involves creating a framework or skeleton for a building that supports the entire structure and ensures its stability, safety, and longevity. Structural design encompasses various elements and considerations, including materials, load-bearing capacity, environmental factors, and architectural aesthetics.
